Travel to Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is Central Asia country, which also known as "Bridge between Asia and Europe". It has a magnificent surrounding: begin with Deserts and Steppes to Mountains and Forests. One of the place for tourists is Almaty. It's a big city for opportunities and relaxation. Could check Shymbylak resort, Kok-Tobe heights.
